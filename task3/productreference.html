Sarong Pareo Beachwear Bikini

LOW MOQS (100 PCS/ORDER) – LUXE QUALITY We are Bali’s leading swimwear manufacturer for low minimum productions – and work with 100+ labels yearly worldwide. These include startup and established brands in USA, Europe and Australia. We are a Swedish, Australian and Indonesian team with 25+ years of experience in the garment and swimwear manufacturing industry. GUIDANCE WHEN NEEDED We will gladly guide you as much or as little as you’d like through our comprehensive and transparent swimwear production process. If you have questions, and are new to this – we’ll guide you all the way. Our mission has always been to help independent entrepreneurs bring their swimwear dreams to life, with reduced impact on the environment. ALWAYS ETHICAL – ECO OPTIONS We pride ourselves on offering you ethical swimwear manufacturing at a good price point; and include recycled and sustainable ECONYL® and REPREVE® fabrics in our range. You can choose eco options of almost everything we offer making a fully sustainable swim line. On our end we donate proceeds of our profits for ocean cleanup as one of many initiatives for sustainable manufacturing. Our working conditions are in many regards to western standards. SWIMSUIT SPECIALISTS – BIKINI NERDS We have the know-how to simplify the journey for you – from design to shop by offering you a one-stop-shop solution including sourcing, sample development, bulk production, quality control and everything else until finished product at your door. Our manufacturing pathways encompass two major private label options: your custom designs and our ready designs. With ready design we launch new designs seasonally that you can put your label on with your own tweaks. You can also combine our designs with yours. This makes starting a swim line so much easier.
